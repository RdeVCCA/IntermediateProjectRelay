About Us

Welcome to Los Pollos Hermanos, where we serve the finest chicken cooked to perfection. Our story began in Albuquerque, New Mexico, with a single goal in mind: to provide affordable, delicious, and authentic meals in a family-friendly environment. Founded by Gustavo Fring, Los Pollos Hermanos has grown from a small eatery to a beloved chain known for its signature flavors and quick service.

At Los Pollos Hermanos, quality is our priority. We use only the freshest ingredients and our unique blend of spices to give you a taste that you won't forget. Our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring that each customer leaves satisfied, with a smile on their face and a desire to come back again.

Thank you for choosing Los Pollos Hermanos - where something delicious is always cooking!
